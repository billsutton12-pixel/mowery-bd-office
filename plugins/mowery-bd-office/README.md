# Mowery BD Office

A five-agent business-development crew for Bill Sutton at Mowery Construction. Dispatch any unit by its call sign — "Scout, find me five in the Lehigh Valley," "Chase, what's going cold?", "Sage, where should we focus?"

## The crew

| Call sign | Role | What it owns |
|---|---|---|
| **Scout** | Prospector | Fills the top of the funnel — sources, qualifies (6-factor score), writes deals into HubSpot, opens intros |
| **Chase** | Progressor | Keeps every open deal moving with stage- and construction-timeline-aware decision-maker touches |
| **Quill** | Collateral | Builds brand-locked qualifications packages and RFP responses, with a verify-before-submission checklist |
| **Sage** | Analyst | Ranks where the team wins and profits (hit rate × margin) and flags misallocated effort |
| **Ace** | Chief of staff | Triages the inbox, decides when a meeting is warranted, and proposes your best open slots |

## The relay

Scout fills the funnel → Chase advances the deals → Quill packages them when a proposal moment hits. Sage steers the whole board; Ace keeps your desk clear. Each unit takes one dispatch at a time — you (or Sage) hand off the baton.

## Autonomy posture

Internal, low-risk actions (HubSpot notes, tasks, records; email filing) run unattended. Anything a client or decision maker sees is staged as a draft for one-tap approval — the crew never sends to a decision maker on its own.
